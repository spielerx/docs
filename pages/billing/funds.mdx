# Manage Funds

<Callout type="warning">
  Manage Funds is not applicable for Solana.
</Callout>

## Deposit Funds

To make sure your subscription is renewed every month, you need to deposit funds to the Spheron escrow wallet. \
Follow these steps to deposit funds:

1. Connect your wallet if it is not connected.
2. Select **Deposit Funds** from the **Connect Wallet** dropdown.
3. Add the amount you want to approve and Click "Approve."
4. Add the amount you want to deposit and Click "Deposit."

## Withdraw Funds

Spheron allows you to withdraw funds from the Spheron escrow wallet if you no longer wish to pay for the subscription. You can do this anytime. \
Follow these steps to withdraw funds:

1. Connect your wallet if it is not connected.
2. Select **Withdraw Funds** from the **connect wallet** dropdown.
3. Enter the amount you want to withdraw and Click "Withdraw."

<Callout type="info">
  **NOTE:** \
  An insufficient balance in the Spheron escrow wallet may stop all
  the running services. You can find the details of all the deposit and
  withdrawal transactions in the **Payment History** section under the **Billing** tab.
</Callout>
